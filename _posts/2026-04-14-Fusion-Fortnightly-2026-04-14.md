## Government

[Ridgway Machines appoints Mike Allen as new Managing Director](https://tokamakenergy.com/2026/04/02/ridgway-machines-appoints-mike-allen-as-new-md/). Tokamak Energy [announced in September 2025](https://tokamakenergy.com/2025/09/22/tokamak-energy-acquires-ridgway-machines-to-accelerate-growth-of-superconducting-business/) that is was aquiring Ridgeway Machines to help grow its superconducting magnet business. scale automated production systems for high-temperature superconducting (HTS) cabling and coils

[Commonwealth Fusion Systems and Realta Fusion announced a  long-term strategic partnership for the design and manufacturing of high-temperature superconducting (HTS) magnets.](https://cfs.energy/news-and-media/realta-fusion-and-commonwealth-fusion-systems-form-strategic-partnership-to-commercialize-magnetic-mirror-fusion-energy/) Commonwealth Fusion Systems will design and manufacture high‑temperature superconducting magnet systems for Realta’s magnetic‑mirror fusion prototypes and future commercial plants, an agreement described as potentially reaching multi‑billion‑dollar value. The release also described a talent‑sharing element in which CFS expertise is intended to support magnet design, manufacturing, deployment, and operations for magnetic‑mirror systems.

[TAE Technologies announced the completion of a site evaluation tour across Alabama, Ohio, and Texas.](https://tae.com/tae-technologies-completes-multi-state-site-evaluation-tour-for-first-fusion-power-plant/) It conducted on‑site assessments and stakeholder meetings focused on infrastructure readiness, grid connectivity, land access, workforce, and incentives.

[Helion released the first-round of 2026 awards under its HERCULES program.](https://www.helionenergy.com/articles/helion-hercules-fusion-research-funding/) Helion allocated $4 million of funding across 25 proposals at 20 institutions spanning the United States and the United Kingdom. Helion framed the awards as targeting enabling technologies for scaling commercial fusion—explicitly including materials, magnet systems, and diagnostics—and stated that it has more than $17 million committed to the program through 2028, with another application phase planned for mid‑2026.

[Avalanche Energy was awarded a $5.2 million contract from DARPA to develop compact alpha‑voltaic technology.](https://www.avalanchefusion.com/news-release/avalanche-energy-awarded-5-2m-darpa-contract-to-develop-radioisotope-power-technology) It is a 30‑month effort to mature micro‑fabricated cells that convert alpha particle energy directly into electricity.

[Commonwealth Fusion Systems now completed about three quarters of the work to build SPARC.](https://blog.cfs.energy/our-sparc-fusion-facility-is-now-about-75-done-take-a-virtual-tour-of-the-progress/)

[Wisconsin enacted 2025 Wisconsin Act 165, creating a sales-and-use tax exemption for qualifying “nuclear fusion technology projects”.](https://docs.legis.wisconsin.gov/document/acts/2025/165) It explicitly defined eligible projects broadly (e.g., controlled fusion and related R&D including energy generation, propulsion systems, materials research, medical isotope production, neutron sources, and other fusion-enabling applications). This is a state-level industrial policy move intended to reduce project costs and pull fusion development and supply-chain activity into Wisconsin.

[U.S. Department of Energy’s Office of Energy Dominance Financing announced a conditional financing commitment linked to SHINE’s domestic molybdenum‑99 medical-isotope production effort.](https://www.energy.gov/articles/does-office-energy-dominance-financing-announces-conditional-commitment-domestic-medical) Federal finances are being used to de-risk fusion-adjacent industrial infrastructure tied to neutron-source technology, specialized manufacturing, and regulated isotope supply chains.

[Canada’s Department of Finance published its Green Bond Allocation and Impact Report 2024–25, reporting that eligible green-bond proceeds were allocated to certain nuclear-energy expenditures.](https://www.canada.ca/en/department-finance/programs/financial-sector-policy/securities/debt-program/green-bond-allocation-impact-report-2024-25.html) It explicitly listed “the advancement of General Fusion’s fusion energy systems towards integrated prototype readiness” among nuclear-related projects.

[France and Japan issued a joint declaration on nuclear-energy cooperation that includes an explicit commitment to continue cooperation on fusion-energy projects, including ITER and JT‑60SA.](https://www.elysee.fr/emmanuel-macron/2026/04/01/declaration-conjointe-franco-japonaise-sur-la-cooperation-dans-le-domaine-de-lenergie-nucleaire) They reiterate key program timelines (commissioning JT‑60SA by end‑2026 and starting ITER’s research operations phase in 2034), reinforcing governmental backing for the public fusion R&D stack and for the long-lead industrial supply chains supporting these flagship systems.

[The U.S. Department of Energy published its FY 2027 Budget in Brief.](https://www.energy.gov/documents/doe-fy-2027-budget-brief) It includes a dedicated Fusion Energy Sciences section setting out requested funding and program direction—an in-window federal budget-positioning action that matters for commercialization-adjacent R&D, national-lab and university performers, and companies positioning for federally funded collaborations and procurement down the fusion supply chain.

[European Commission Executive Vice-President Teresa Ribera visited Fusion for Energy](https://fusionforenergy.europa.eu/news/vice-president-of-the-european-commission-teresa-ribera-visits-f4e-in-barcelona/) (the EU’s joint undertaking for ITER and fusion development) for a senior-level engagement focused on fusion’s industrial and commercial benefits and Europe’s large ITER-linked supplier base, an EU governance-and-industrial-policy signal that situates fusion supply-chain capacity and commercialization readiness within high-level Commission priorities.
